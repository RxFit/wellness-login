Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

RxFit AI, Inc. ("RxFit," "we," "us," or "our") operates the RxFit Wellness mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

When you use the RxFit Wellness App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials used to sign in to your account.
  • Apple HealthKit Data: With your explicit permission, we read health and fitness data from Apple Health, including:
    • Vitals & Cardiovascular: Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), respiratory rate, and VO2 max.
    • Body Composition: Body mass (weight), body fat percentage, and lean body mass.
    • Activity & Movement: Step count, active and basal energy burned, walking/running distance, exercise minutes, stand time, and flights climbed.
    • Gait & Mobility: Walking heart rate average, walking speed, stride length, walking asymmetry (left/right gait balance), stair ascent speed, stair descent speed, and six-minute walk test distance.
    • Nutrition: Dietary energy consumed (calories), protein, carbohydrates, total fat, sugar, fiber, cholesterol, sodium, iron, potassium, and caffeine.
    • Hydration: Water intake.
    • Sleep: Sleep analysis including sleep stages (deep, REM, core, awake).
    • Workouts: Workout type, duration, distance, and energy burned.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

How We Use Apple HealthKit Data

We use HealthKit data solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized wellness coaching insights to you and your assigned coach.
  • Tracking heart rate zones during workouts to optimize training intensity.
  • Monitoring recovery through sleep quality and HRV analysis.
  • Reviewing nutrition and macronutrient intake to optimize fueling strategies.
  • Tracking hydration status for performance and workplace safety.
  • Analyzing gait and mobility to detect asymmetry and injury risk.
  • Helping your wellness coach create and adjust your training program.

We do not:

  • Sell HealthKit data to third parties.
  • Use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Share HealthKit data with third-party analytics or advertising platforms.
  • Store HealthKit data in iCloud.
  • Write any data to Apple Health. The App only reads your health data.

Data Storage and Security

Your health data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ and stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Session credentials are stored securely on your device using the iOS Keychain (for biometric login) and Capacitor Preferences API.

Data Retention

We retain your health data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you with wellness coaching services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Sharing

Your health data may be shared with:

  • Your Assigned Coach: The wellness coach assigned to you by your employer's program can view your health metrics to provide personalized guidance.
  • Your Employer's Program Administrator: Aggregated, de-identified data may be shared with program administrators. Individual health data is never shared with your employer.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.
  • Disconnect HealthKit integration within the App.
  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Delete your account (see below).
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the App.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To delete your account:

  • Open the App, tap your profile, and select "Delete Account"; or
  • Email privacy@rxfit.ai with the subject line "Delete My Account."

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove your account credentials, health data, coaching records, and any other personal information within 30 days. This action is irreversible.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
