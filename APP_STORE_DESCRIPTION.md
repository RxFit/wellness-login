## Description

RxFit Wellness brings personalized, AI-driven wellness coaching to Industrial Athletes — the people who power the physical workforce.

Connect your Apple Health data to unlock insights tailored to your body and your job. RxFit tracks the metrics that matter most for physically demanding roles:

- Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep duration and quality
- VO2 Max and respiratory rate
- Workouts and active energy
- Step count and daily movement

Your coach uses this data to build a complete picture of your recovery, readiness, and long-term health trends — so you can perform at your best and stay injury-free.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Sign in with your RxFit account
2. Connect Apple Health to sync your health data automatically
3. Your AI-powered coach analyzes your trends and delivers personalized guidance
4. Data syncs in the background so your insights are always up to date

BUILT FOR THE WORKFORCE

RxFit Wellness is designed for organizations that invest in the health of their teams. Whether you work in construction, manufacturing, logistics, or any physically demanding field, RxFit helps you take control of your wellness with data-driven coaching.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your health data is encrypted and transmitted securely. We only read the data types you authorize, and your information is never sold or shared with third parties.

## App Review Notes (for Apple's review team)

This app provides native Apple HealthKit integration that enables our AI wellness coaching platform to deliver personalized health insights. The web platform at app.rxfit.ai cannot access HealthKit data directly — the native iOS app bridges this gap by reading authorized health data types and securely syncing them to the coaching platform.

Core native functionality:
- HealthKit authorization and data reading (heart rate, HRV, sleep, VO2 Max, workouts, steps, respiratory rate, and more)
- Background delivery via observer queries for real-time data syncing
- Secure session management with cookie-based authentication
